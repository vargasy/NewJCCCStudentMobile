Salon & Spa Services

The JCCC School of Cosmetology offers to students and the community discounted rates for a variety of salon services, including haircuts, manicures, and facials. JCCC students with a valid student ID receive an additional 20% discount. All services in the school are performed by students under the supervision of a licensed cosmetology instructor.
