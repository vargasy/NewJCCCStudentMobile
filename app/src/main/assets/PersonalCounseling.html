Personal Counseling

JCCC counselors provide a safe and confidential environment to talk with you about any personal issues or emotional difficulties you may experience. If necessary, we can refer you to the student assistance program at St. Luke's Health System. The St. Luke's program is not free of charge, so any costs will be discussed with you prior to being referred.
