Student Lounge

Looking a place to relax before your next class?

Want to play video games, card games or board games? JCCC has over 50 games for Xbox 360 with Kinect, PlayStation 3 and Nintendo Wii, and new games are purchased throughout the year to stay updated on the most popular titles.

Foosball or ping-pong more your style?

We have those, too. We also have over a dozen board games, including Scattergories, Apples to Apples and Settlers of Cattan. Come to the Student Lounge in COM 322 and hang out with friends or make new ones.

Events and Tournaments

Each semester we hold multiple activities in the Student Lounge. All students are welcome! Some of our past events include:

  • Nerf Basketball Shoot-out
  • Guesstimation Station!
  • Lavish Luau
  • Call of Duty Tournament
  • Ping-Pong Tournament
