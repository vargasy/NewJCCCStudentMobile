Hiersteiner Child

Development Center

The center is licensed to care for children who are age 1 and walking to age 6. Child care and education are available on a full-time or part-time basis. Enrollment is currently open to children of JCCC students, employees and the community.

Hours:
    Mon.-Thu. 6:30 a.m.-6:30 p.m.
    Fri. 6:30 a.m. to 6 p.m.

The HCDC is located south of the Horticultural Science Center on South Campus Drive. For details about care, enrollment and tuition, visit the HCDC web page.

Location: S. Campus Drive
